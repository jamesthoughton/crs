Mission

Cancer Research Society is a club dedicated to organizing cancer research at TJ, raising awareness for cancer at TJ alongside Relay for Life, aswell as educating our peers to inspire them to perform and share their own research. It is our goal to encourage collaborative research between students at TJ and to provide the foundational knowledge and resources to do so.
